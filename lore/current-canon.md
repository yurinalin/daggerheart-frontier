## Premise in Play

The campaign begins with a frontier expedition pushing into lands that have only recently become accessible as the continent’s ancient storms weaken.

The player characters are members of the **Guilda dos Solucionadores de Problemas**, attached to an expedition based at **Camp Halcyon**.

They are not the most prestigious team available, but they are positioned to make an important discovery.

---

## What Is Publicly True in the Setting

The world has endured brutal climate conditions for longer than living history can clearly explain.

Only a limited region remained suitable for sustained civilization.

Over the last century, the climate has slowly improved and become more predictable.

Expeditions now push deeper into newly accessible territories, searching for safe routes, resources, ruins, and answers about the lost world.

Ancient structures are beginning to emerge from the land.

---

## What the Players Are Expected to Know at Start

- They are part of a frontier expedition.
- Camp Halcyon is a newly established base at the edge of the known world.
- The frontier is dangerous, unstable, and politically important.
- Strange ruins and artifacts are being discovered.
- Their guild handles dangerous, unusual, or unpredictable problems.

---

## Hidden Truths Not Yet Revealed

- The ancient storms were caused by the Auric Dominion through the Tempest Protocol.
- The Varkesh Empire once dominated the center of the continent.
- The Auric Dominion was a coastal magocratic state that entered stasis underground.
- The rediscovered ruins are part of Auric Dominion infrastructure.
- The players may trigger the first awakening signal.

These truths should not be exposed in player-facing material unless intentionally revealed in play.

## Starter Arc Direction

The current intended starter arc is:

1. arrival at the frontier
2. expedition pressure and early complications
3. discovery of an ancient ruin
4. activation of a Dominion climate node
5. confrontation with a guardian or defensive system
6. celebration of a historic discovery
7. hidden awakening begins elsewhere

This structure may change after session zero.
