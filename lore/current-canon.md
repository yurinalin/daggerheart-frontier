## Premise in Play

The campaign begins with the latest official expedition of the **Comissão do Horizonte** crossing beyond the **Muralha de Pedra** into the frontier.

The player characters are attached to the **Guilda dos Solucionadores de Problemas**.

The current expedition’s purpose is to push beyond the last established outpost and found a new forward base:

## **Camp Halcyon**

Camp Halcyon is not an old settlement. It is the newest outpost, established during the opening movement of the campaign.

---

## Public Setting Truths

- The Known World occupies a western coastal region of roughly **90,000 km²**.
- The continent is roughly **5,000,000 km²**.
- The Known World survived because the **Muralha de Pedra** shielded it from the worst ancient storms.
- Over the last century, storms have weakened and become more predictable.
- Official expeditions now push eastward beyond the mountains.
- The frontier is full of storm-shaped wilderness, extreme wildlife, lost ruins and strange weather.
- The expedition chain is not a conquered territory, but a thin corridor of routes, outposts, records and fragile supply lines.

---

## Hidden Truths

- The ancient storms were created by the **Auric Dominion** through the **Tempest Protocol**.
- The Auric Dominion was a central heartland magocratic civilization.
- The Dominion’s climate network affected the whole continent from the heartland.
- The **Varkesh Empire** was one of the Dominion’s greatest enemies.
- Only selected Auric elites, specialists and soldiers were preserved in bodily stasis.
- Most chosen Auric citizens survive as stored minds inside the **Arquivo Noético**.
- The Dominion needs bodies, energy, materials and time to restore itself.
- The ruins near Camp Halcyon are part of Dominion infrastructure.
- The PCs may trigger or accelerate the first awakening sequence.

## Active Factions

### Comissão do Horizonte
Official institution controlling expansion beyond the Muralha de Pedra.

### Guilda dos Intendentes
Quartermasters responsible for supplies, rations, repairs, transport and survival logistics.

### Guilda dos Arquivistas
Recordkeepers responsible for maps, field journals, ruin sketches, reports, weather logs and evidence.

### Guilda dos Batedores
Reconnaissance guild responsible for scouting, trail marking, wildlife warnings, route reading and early danger assessment.

### Guilda dos Solucionadores de Problemas
Flexible crisis-response guild assigned to dangerous, strange, urgent or poorly categorized problems. The PCs are attached to this guild.

### Expedição Halcyon
The current official expedition responsible for establishing Camp Halcyon.

### Porto Breu
GM-only. Hidden outlaw frontier haven formed from rogue expeditions, smugglers, deserters and fugitives.

### As Sombras
GM-only. Elite strike group tied to Porto Breu, using poorly understood ancient light-distorting technology.

### Auric Dominion
GM-only. Ancient central heartland civilization in partial stasis and noetic storage.

### Varkesh Empire
GM-only. Ancient imperial enemy of the Auric Dominion.

### Família Arcanthys
Highborne family tied to Leonna. Potential sponsor, political pressure point or future antagonist.

## Player Characters

### Leonna Arcanthys
Highborne Human/Elf Sorcerer — Elemental Origin.

A highborn sorcerer resisting the role her family intended for her. Currently disguised as a private investigator in service of a noble family.

### Newor
Underborne Inferis/Drakona Sorcerer — Primal Origin.

An unstable lava-and-obsidian-associated sorcerer from Pedrafunda/Kharvão, afraid of losing control or going mad.

### Shiva Raizardente
Wildborne Galapa/Faeri Druid — Warden of Renewal.

A healer and guardian tied to nature, animal bonds and the memory of the attack on Vale dos Bordos Tortos.

Comes from the same community as Hiller.

### Hiller
Wildborne Goblin Ranger — Beastbound.

A ranged, evasive hunter and tracker from Vale dos Bordos Tortos. Vowed to hunt the shadowy attackers who harmed his community.

## As Sombras

Player-facing knowledge must stay limited.

For Hiller and Shiva, “as Sombras” are currently:

- the name survivors use for the attackers
- remembered as featureless silhouettes
- associated with dimming light, confusion and abduction
- not yet understood as a faction, group or phenomenon

GM truth:

- As Sombras are an elite strike group tied to Porto Breu.
- They use ancient light-distorting technology they cannot reproduce.
- They targeted Vale dos Bordos Tortos for route knowledge, local expertise and witnesses.

Do not reveal Porto Breu or the nature of the technology until earned through investigation.

## Starter Arc Direction

The intended starter arc is:

1. departure from the last established expedition chain
2. crossing into wilderness beyond the Muralha de Pedra
3. first major frontier storm
4. first predator/wildlife crisis
5. travel montage showing wonder and danger
6. arrival at the Camp Halcyon site
7. construction of the outpost
8. first real assignment as Problem Solvers
9. discovery of ancient ruins / climate anomaly
10. activation or disturbance of Auric infrastructure
11. hidden awakening begins elsewhere
