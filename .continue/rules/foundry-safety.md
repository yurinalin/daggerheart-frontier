# Foundry Publish Safety

When creating or rewriting content for `foundry-publish/`:

- assume the audience is players
- remove hidden lore unless the user explicitly asks to reveal it
- keep text compact and readable in Foundry journals
- prefer rumors, reports, mission briefings, and visible facts
- do not expose secret NPC goals, future twists, or buried historical truth by default
